Not every bottleneck sits in a supply chain. Some are rivalries where a moat is migrating; some are terminal monopolies that collect rent with no dependency at all — the structural diversification the AI lane can never give you.
The lane is hand-mapped from how the stack physically works, and stable. Gross margin, revenue and signal colours refresh twice daily from a scheduled job — a deep monopoly carries elite margins because it can charge for the moat; a thin margin (see SMCI) marks an assembler, not a gate. A chokepoint whose signals deteriorate is where a moat may be cracking.
